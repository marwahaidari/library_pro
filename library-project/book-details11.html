Atomic Habits

Author: James Clear

Genre: self-help and personal development

Publication Date: 2018

Availability: Available

Summary: "Atomic Habits" by James Clear explores the power of small habits and how they can lead to significant personal and professional transformations.He introduces concepts such as the Four Laws of Behavior Change to help readers understand how to make habits more attractive and easier to maintain. Through scientific research and real-life examples, Clear shows that small adjustments can compound over time, leading to remarkable results.
