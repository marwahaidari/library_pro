The Forty Rules Of Love

Author: Elef Shafak

Genre: Novel

Publication Date: January 1, 2021

Availability: Available

Summary: "The Forty Rules of Love" by Elif Shafak features two intertwined narratives: one in the 13th century with poet Rumi and his mentor Shams of Tabriz, and the other in modern times with Ella Rubenstein, a housewife who discovers Rumi's poetry. As Ella learns about Rumi and Shams' profound relationship, she reflects on her own struggles with love and self-discovery. The novel delves into themes of spirituality and the quest for meaning through Shams' "forty rules," illustrating how love transcends time and transforms lives.
