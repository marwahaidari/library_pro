The Midnight Library

Author: Matt Haig

Genre: Novel

Publication Date: January 1, 2021

Availability: Available

Summary:"The Midnight Library" by Matt Haig follows Nora Seed, who discovers a magical library filled with books representing different lives she could have lived. As she explores these alternate realities, she confronts her regrets and learns valuable lessons about choice, fulfillment, and the importance of living in the present.
